Frequently Asked Questions

General
What is FreshoKartz?

FreshoKartz is fastest growing vegetables Delivery Company which helps you order all kind of fruits and vegetables that you need every day from your cell phone.

What cities and locations do you operate in?

FreshoKartz currently operates in Jaipur. For more details on where we work, please visit Operation page of our website.

Do you deliver to my location?

We deliver in select localities across the cities we are present in. You can call us/visit operations page to check if we deliver in your area.

What is the minimum order value?

There is no minimum order value. However, right now we only deliver to Hotels, Restaurants, Dhabas and Hostels.

Do you charge any amount or taxes over and above the rates?

No, we do not charge anything over and above the rates. However, we do have a delivery fee in case you want express delivery.

What is express delivery?

Generally we take orders one day before delivery time, in case you want same day delivery within 3 hours, express service charge of Rs. 50/- will be levied.

Placing an order
How can I be sure the vegetables I order are of good quality?

We have a quality check process in place to ensure quality of the items delivered is up to the mark. But if you find items that are not up to your expectation, please do let us know we will bring the one that matches.

How will I know if any item in my order is unavailable?

You will receive an SMS notification informing you about the unavailable items in this situation. Refunds (if any) will also be initiated within 4-6 hours.

I’m trying to place an order today but it is getting scheduled for the next day. What can I do?

We usually schedule delivery for the next day of order being placed. However, you can choose express delivery for same day delivery if you place an order before 4PM.

Can I schedule an order to my convenience?

Sure. At the checkout page, you can select a delivery slot of your choice or if you are ordering on call just tell us your preferred time and location.

Delivery related queries
Do you charge for delivery?

No, we don’t charge for delivery.

What are your delivery times?

We begin deliveries in some locations at 7AM and complete the last delivery by 6PM.

Can I change the delivery address of my order?

Yes, you can change your delivery address before the order has been picked for delivery. Once the order has been picked, you will have to cancel and reorder for the other location.

What if I don’t receive my order by the scheduled time?

On rare occasions, due to unforeseen circumstances, your order might be delayed. In case of imminent delay, our customer support executive will keep you updated about the delivery time of your order. We will work with you to make sure you are not inconvenienced as a result of the delay.

Payments related queries
How can I make payments at FreshoKartz?

FreshoKartz accepts multiple modes of payment. You can make online payments using credit cards, debit cards, net banking, PayTM Wallet and bank cheque. Cash on delivery (COD) is also available for orders less than INR 10000.

Cancellation and Returns
How can I cancel my order?

FreshoKartz provides easy and hassle free cancellation. You can cancel your order by calling us before it is dispatched to your home.

What If I want to return something?

If you’re dissatisfied with the products delivered, please register a complaint via the app or call us within 12 hours. Our customer support team will get in touch with you to resolve this issue.

Can I reschedule my order?

Yes, you can do this by calling us. Your order can be rescheduled to your convenience as per the options available provided it is not already enroute.

What if I have any complaint regarding my order?

You can use the Contact Us section on the app/web or give us a call to reach us. Our customer care executives are always happy to help.
